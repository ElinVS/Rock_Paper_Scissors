Rules of the game

A classic two-person game. Players start each round by saying, “rock, paper, scissors, shoot!” On “shoot,” each player holds out their fist for rock, flat hand for paper, or their index and middle finger for scissors.

In this virtual example it is you agains the computer. Select your choice of rock,paper or scissors from the dropdown menu and hit the "shoot" button! Go into Play to start the game

Rock crushes scissors
Paper covers rock
Scissors cut paper
